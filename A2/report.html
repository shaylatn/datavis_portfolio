Subtheme: Exclusionary Zoning

Overall Analysis Questions

  1. What are the income differences between largely single family zoned areas and other housing areas? In some of the readings from A1, it stated that single family housing is typically advertised for older, middle to upper class families, so I wanted to see what this income gap looked like.
  2. Which racial demographics are most prevalent in Massachusetts? Historically, I know there have been zoning laws made to exclude certain demographics from living in an area, so I wanted to see if there were any trends.
  3. What is the age distribution of each type of housing area? From the reading it seemed like inner cities like NY had more dense housing. I feel like younger individuals often live in inner cities while older adults with families tend to move to the suburbs where there are more single family homes.
